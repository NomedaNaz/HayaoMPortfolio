WHAT I DO

As a co-founder of Studio Ghibli, I have brought to life unforgettable worlds and characters through unique blend of hand-drawn animation and imaginative narratives. My work often explores themes of nature, humanity, and the complexities of life, captivating audiences of all ages.

With a career spanning over five decades, my films such as "My Neighbor Totoro," "Spirited Away," and "Princess Mononoke" have left an indelible mark on the world of animation, earning numerous awards and critical acclaim.

Hayao Miyazaki-
